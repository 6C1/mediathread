Work in Mediathread centers around Items, Projects, and Discussions:

Items are images or videos that have been saved and annotated in MediaThread.

  • Media sources must first be imported into MediaThread before they can be saved as Items.
  • Many videos and images posted online may be imported into MediaThread.
  • To prioritize media sources for your course, you can queue up supported collections or import a core group of Items for analysis. See instructions in the Explore Collections tab.
  • All Items in MediaThread are available to all members of the class.

Projects are written compositions with embedded Items.

  • Any MediaThread user can create a project and add collaborators.
  • A project may be saved as a private draft, in which case it is visible only to its author(s). It may also be published and made visible to the instructor, the whole class, or the world.
  • Instructors have another publishing option: they may save a project as an "assignment" for the class, so responses can be tracked here, in the Instructor Dashboard.
  • Projects may be discussed by Instructors posting "Instructor Feedback". Class members are alerted to Feedback posted on their Project(s).
  • Any Project created by an instructor and made visible to the class, whether or not it is an assignment, will appear on the Class Home.

Discussions are conversations about specific topics.

  • Topic-level discussions are accessible to the whole class but can only be created by instructors here in the Instructor Dashboard.
  • Once created, topic-level Discussions appear on the Class Home page.
